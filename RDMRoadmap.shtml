Oxford's Research Data Management Dashboard

Research Data Management (RDM) is a crucial part of any research project. With the increased pressure on publically funded research to be made available Open Access and for all data behind publications to also be available (the Open Data movement), there is significant pressure on Universities to invest in RDM infrastructure.

University of Oxford's Policy on Research Data and Records lays out the responsibilities of both researchers and the University. The University has invested over a decade to develop a sophisticated knowledge and technology stack to enure that the data behind publications is securely backed up, consistently citeable and available longterm subject to relevant Open Access guidelines.
